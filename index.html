About House of Chops

House of Chops is an online catering company currently servicing Warri and it's environs and we have a vision of expanding our operation to every part of Nigeria in no time. It was born out of passion to provide, quality, affordable and mouth watering food to the good people of Warri and beyond. We are specialists in Continental, African Dishes and everything in between.

Our operation is very simple, you place your order and we deliver to you within 3hrs. We have fresh Banga, Egusi, Oha, Owho Soup. Fresh fish Stew, Chicken Stew, Offada Stew, Jellof rice, fried rice, Isi Ewu, Nkwobi and many many other kinds of food

Our food come in 3 and 4litres pack and we also have outdoor catering services available for your birthday parties, wedding parties etc. We promise to give you a taste of mama's bottom pot. Kindly place your order with any of our channels
